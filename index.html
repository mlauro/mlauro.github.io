Baking and Design

This is a collection of recipes which includes baking tips, cooking process, and aesthetic in each tasty treat. I was inspired by my new-found love for baking; these are some of the recipes I want to try or have tried. Moreover, these can help challenge designers to create something beyond taste and to further learn about design elements such as texture, colour, breadth, and shape. The abundance of recipes and innovative ideas are out there, and hopefully continues to get added to this webpage.
